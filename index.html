</> she hacks sydney

An hack day event open to all women developers in Sydney.

Who?

She Hacks Sydney will be the first of its kind and aims at encouraging more women to participate in entrepreneurship, hackdays and technical events in Sydney. It will be a great opportunity to team up, meet some new people and learn some new skills in a rapid timeframe.

What?

Attendees will team up to work on an idea they are passionate about and build a 'hack' or rapid prototype. At the end of the hack event, each group will present their ideas and the judges will pick winners.

When?

At this stage, our idea is in incubation stage and we need to secure some sponsorship. We expect to hold this event either late 2013 or early 2013. Watch this space.
